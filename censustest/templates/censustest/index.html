CensusTest

CensusTest takes data from the 2011 Canadian census and lets users answer simple questions to see how they compare to other Canadians.

Take the test.

In under two minutes, you can answer census questions related to topics like Demographics, Culture and Work. The test is completely anonymous.

Compare.

After completing the test, you can view stats and charts to see how your answers compare to the rest of Canada.

Take the test

About

CensusTest gets it's data from the 2011 Census Profile and the 2011 National Household Survey Profile which are publicly available from the Canadian government on the Statistics Canada website.

On the technical side, CensusTest is implemented as a Django web application and uses PostgreSQL as a database. The code is available here
